Privacy Policy for Jendo Health

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Welcome to Jendo Health ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: By using Jendo Health, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and password
  • Health Information: Medical records, test results, health conditions, medications, and wellness data
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, health preferences, and notification settings

1.2 Camera and Media Access

Why We Need Camera Permission:

  • Document Scanning: To capture and upload medical documents, prescriptions, and lab reports
  • Profile Photos: To allow you to take profile pictures directly from the app
  • Medical Records: To photograph physical medical records for digital storage
  • Telemedicine: To enable video consultations with healthcare providers

How We Use Camera Data:

  • Photos and videos are processed locally on your device
  • Images are only uploaded to our secure servers with your explicit consent
  • We do not access your camera without your permission
  • You can revoke camera access at any time through your device settings
  • We do not use camera data for advertising or third-party purposes

1.3 Storage Access

We request storage permissions to:

  • Save medical documents and reports to your device
  • Access and upload existing medical documents from your device
  • Cache data for offline access

1.4 Microphone Access

We request microphone access for:

  • Voice consultations with healthcare providers
  • Audio notes for health tracking
  • Voice commands in the wellness chatbot

1.5 Network and Internet Access

Required for:

  • Syncing your health data across devices
  • Communicating with healthcare providers
  • Downloading test results and medical reports
  • Accessing online health resources and learning materials

1.6 Notifications

We collect notification preferences to send you:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Medication reminders
  • Test result notifications
  • Important health updates

1.7 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: Error logs, performance data, and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Health Services: Providing personalized health tracking, medical record management, and wellness recommendations
  • Doctor Consultations: Facilitating communication between you and healthcare providers
  • Appointment Management: Scheduling and reminding you of appointments
  • Test Results: Delivering and storing laboratory and medical test results
  • Wellness Programs: Providing diet, exercise, sleep, and stress management guidance
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries and providing technical assistance
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app functionality and user experience
  • Security: Protecting against unauthorized access and fraudulent activities
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws and regulations, including healthcare privacy laws

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 With Healthcare Providers

We share your health information with doctors and healthcare providers that you choose to consult through our app, with your explicit consent.

3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Cloud storage and hosting services
  • Analytics and app performance monitoring
  • Payment processing
  • Customer support services

These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Jendo Health, our users, or others.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

3.5 With Your Consent

We will not share your personal health information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Health data is stored on secure, encrypted servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to your information on a need-to-know basis
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication options to protect your account
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • HIPAA Compliance: We adhere to HIPAA standards for health information protection (where applicable)

Note: While we take extensive measures to secure your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide you with our services
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., medical record retention laws)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. However, we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information through the app's profile settings at any time.

6.2 Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal health information in a portable format.

6.3 Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Please note that some information may be retained as required by law.

6.4 Permission Management

You can manage app permissions (camera, microphone, storage, notifications) through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Apps → Jendo Health → Permissions

Revoking certain permissions may limit app functionality.

6.5 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link or adjusting notification preferences in the app.

6.6 Do Not Sell My Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Jendo Health is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians may contact us if they believe their child has provided us with information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification

Your continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
